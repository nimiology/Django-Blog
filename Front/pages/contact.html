Starting in a country where music is highly restricted, HEEN courageously continues to pursue his passion. He continuously performed DJ sets in various underground shows in Iran, and with each project, he constantly refined his work, striving for perfection to master his craft. Throughout this journey, he connects with Iran’s best hip hop artists and finally starts collaborating with one of Iran’s most renowned artists: Mehrad Hidden Together, they have taken on many projects such as planning album marketing strategies, organizing Iran’s very first Electronic Music Seminar and creating motivational documentaries, and many more. After years of production and hard work, he built his path and created his record label ``Lighthouse Records`` with one of Iran's most talented and expert in the dance music scene MJoobi who had many hits in the Iranian/ Worldwide market.
